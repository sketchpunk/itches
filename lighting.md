### Quantized lights
- https://x.com/TheMirzaBeig/status/1818539915728072789
```
RdotV (specular), quantized before applying light colour.
round(dot(norm, lightDir) * x) / x;
round(dot(reflect(-lightDir, norm), viewDir) * x) / x;
(x = resolution, or steps)
```
